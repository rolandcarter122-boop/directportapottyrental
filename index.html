Frequently Asked Questions

How is odor controlled in your portable toilets?

Odor control is a three-part process: a powerful, biodegradable deodorizing chemical in the tank that breaks down waste and neutralizes odors; engineered ventilation to promote airflow; and, most importantly, regular and thorough professional servicing.

What are the site requirements for placing a unit?

The unit must be placed on a solid, level surface. Our service trucks need a clear access path that is at least 10-12 feet wide, and the truck must be able to get within 25 feet of the unit to properly service it.

What happens if there is severe weather like high winds or heavy rain?

Our units are designed to be stable and weather-resistant. In cases of extreme weather advisories, we may contact you to discuss the safest placement. If a unit does tip over, please call us immediately, and we will dispatch a crew to resolve the situation.

What is included in the rental price?

Our quotes are all-inclusive. The price you see includes the delivery of the unit, placement, pickup, and all scheduled servicing and supplies (like toilet paper and chemicals) for the duration of your rental.

Can I move the porta potty myself after it has been placed?

For your safety and to ensure the unit remains serviceable, we ask that you do not attempt to move it. If you need it relocated, please contact us, and we will send a technician to move it for you safely.
